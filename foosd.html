The Art of Homemade pizza



Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a leavened dough base topped with sauce (often tomato-based), cheese (typically mozzarella), and various other ingredients, then baked at high temperatures. Originating from the flatbread traditions of the ancient Mediterranean, pizza evolved into its modern form in 18th-century Naples and is now a global food with countless variations.
